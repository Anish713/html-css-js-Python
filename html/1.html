Semantic HTML

HTML5 introduced several new semantic elements. Semantic elements are elements with a meaning. SEO reply on it. A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer.
Examples of non-semantic elements: <div> and <span> - Tells nothing about its content.

Examples of semantic elements: <form>, <table>, and <article> - Clearly defines its content.
